SOLVING PROBLEMS AND ATTAIN GOALS

My role as a UX dseigner is to make a product or service usable, enjoyabl, and accesible. UX design is associated with digital design for websites and apps.
I build meaningful experiences for users through my design, my work revolves around branding, User experience-design and visual design. My job is to make sure that users avoid frustrating feelings when a website, app, product is not doing what the user wants it to do. My end goal as a UX designer is to provide the user with intuitive, efficient, and relevant experience,UX design focuses heavily on having a deep understanding of users, what they need. what they value, their abilities, and their limitations, this also takes into account the goals and objectives of my brand.
